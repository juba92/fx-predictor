⬡ Prediction Methodology

Markov Chain

Encodes each H1 candle as Up/Down state. Builds a transition probability matrix from recent sequences to predict the next likely direction.

Weight: 45%

Z-Score / Mean Reversion

Measures deviation from rolling 20-period mean in standard deviations. Extreme values signal probable mean reversion.

Part of indicator score

Indicator Scoring

RSI overbought/oversold + MACD histogram momentum + Bollinger %B — combined into a single [-1, +1] directional score.

Weight: 55%

Kelly Criterion

Optimal position size: f* = p − (1−p)/R where R = TP/SL ratio. Capped at 25% to prevent over-sizing.
